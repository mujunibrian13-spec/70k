================================================================================
                        WHY LAST?
================================================================================

Placing "Group Savings" as the LAST card makes sense because:

1. VISUAL HIERARCHY
   - It's the main KPI (Key Performance Indicator)
   - Placing it last gives it emphasis
   - Viewers' eyes naturally land there at the end

2. LOGICAL FLOW
   - Summary stats (1-2): Members and Other Members
   - Activity stats (3-5): Loans, Applications, Payments
   - Overall health (6): Total Group Savings
   - Follows a natural progression

3. IMPORTANCE
   - The "Group Savings" is the most important metric
   - It represents overall group financial health
   - Should stand out as the culminating metric

4. COMPARISON
   - "Other Members Savings" (Card 2) comes early
   - "Group Savings" (Card 6) comes last
   - Shows progression from partial to complete picture

5. USER EXPERIENCE
   - Natural reading order left to right
   - Important metrics bookend the row
   - Balanced visual weight
