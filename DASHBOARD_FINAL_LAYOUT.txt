================================================================================
                      BREAKDOWN CALCULATION
================================================================================

Total Group Savings: Sum of all members' savings

Breakdown:
  Alison: Aliganyira Alson's individual savings
  Others: Total Group Savings - Alison's Savings

Example:
  Total: 10,500,000 UGX
  Alison: 500,000 UGX
  Others: 10,000,000 UGX (10,500,000 - 500,000)

                    WHY THIS LAYOUT IS BETTER
================================================================================

Benefits of moving Group Savings to member section:

1. LOGICAL GROUPING
   - Main stats: Quick overview (members, money in/out)
   - Member cards: Detailed member-level information
   - Group Savings fits better with member data

2. CONTEXT
   - Shows with Alison's savings and others
   - Provides complete picture of savings composition
   - More meaningful when seen with breakdown

3. FLOW
   - Main stats first (5 cards)
   - Member details second (4 cards)
   - Natural progression from general to specific

4. EMPHASIS
   - Group Savings now among member-focused cards
   - Part of the member analysis section
   - Better visual balance with 4 cards in row

5. READABILITY
   - Cleaner main stats row (5 cards)
   - Balanced member cards row (4 cards)
   - No overwhelming number of cards in one row
