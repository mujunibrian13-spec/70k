MAIN STATISTICS ROW:

1. Total Members
   - Shows: Number of active members
   - Color: Green
   - Icon: Users

2. Other Members Savings ⭐ (NEW POSITION)
   - Shows: Group Savings - Alison's Savings
   - Calculation: Total Group - Alison = Other Members
   - Color: Teal gradient
   - Icon: Chart Pie
   - Subtitle: "Excluding Alison"

3. Group Savings
   - Shows: Total of all members' savings
   - Color: Blue
   - Icon: Piggy Bank
   - Subtitle: "All members combined"

4. Outstanding Loans
   - Shows: Total active & approved loans
   - Color: Red/Danger
   - Icon: Credit Card
   - Subtitle: "Active & approved"

5. Pending Applications
   - Shows: Number of pending loan requests
   - Color: Orange/Warning
   - Icon: Hourglass
   - Subtitle: "Loan applications"

6. Payment Approvals
   - Shows: Number of pending payment approvals
   - Color: Red gradient
   - Icon: Money Bill Check
   - Subtitle: "Pending approval"


MEMBER-SPECIFIC CARDS:

   - Shows: Member details (name, email, savings, loans, interest)

   - Explains: How interest sharing works

================================================================================
                      CARD CALCULATIONS
================================================================================

Other Members Savings Card (NEW POSITION):

Calculation:
  Other Members = Total Group Savings - Alison's Savings
  
Example:
  Total Group Savings: 10,500,000 UGX
  Alison's Savings:      500,000 UGX
  ─────────────────────────────────
  Other Members:      10,000,000 UGX

Updates:
  - Real-time from database
  - Calculated on page load
  - Uses $total_savings and $alison_savings variables
